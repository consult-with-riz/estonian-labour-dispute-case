# Estonia: Know Your Rights After Termination

A free, single-file web tool helping workers in Estonia understand their rights after wrongful termination. Available in English, Estonian, and Russian.

## What it does

Guides users through 8 questions about their termination situation and produces a personalised summary of their legal rights, a step-by-step action plan, a document checklist, and links to official resources — all in plain language.

## Deploy

You can check the tool [here](http://consult-with-riz.github.io/estonian-labour-dispute-case)

## Legal basis

Based on the Estonian Employment Contracts Act (in force 13.02.2026, RT I, 03.02.2026, 5) and the Labour Dispute Resolution Act. Last verified March 2026.

This tool explains the law. It is not legal advice.
